# HESTIA•32 Framework

Nutritional Intelligence System  
A structured framework for nutritional science, metabolic health, and dietary system analysis.

---

## Overview

HESTIA•32 focuses on the science of human nutrition and metabolic health.

Nutrition operates as a complex biological system where diet, metabolism, microbiome dynamics, and lifestyle interact to influence human health outcomes. HESTIA•32 organizes these factors into structured analytical layers that allow dietary systems to be studied, evaluated, and optimized.

The framework assists nutrition professionals in analyzing dietary patterns, metabolic responses, and long-term nutritional health.

---

## Core Engines

HESTIA•32 operates through four analytical engines.

**DIET•32**  
Dietary structure, nutritional intake analysis, and diet pattern evaluation.

**METABOLIC•32**  
Energy systems, metabolism, and biochemical nutritional processes.

**MICROBIOME•32**  
Gut microbiota dynamics and digestive ecosystem analysis.

**LIFESTYLE•32**  
Interaction between nutrition, behavior, physical activity, and long-term health outcomes.

Together these engines allow nutritional systems to be analyzed as integrated biological processes.
