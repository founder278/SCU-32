# CYBER•32 Framework

Cybersecurity Intelligence & Infrastructure Defense System  
A structural architecture for cyber risk analysis, threat monitoring, and infrastructure resilience.

---

## Overview

CYBER•32 proposes a structured approach to cybersecurity intelligence.

Rather than focusing solely on isolated threats, the framework analyzes cyber risk as a system-level phenomenon involving infrastructure exposure, attack surfaces, operational vulnerabilities, and response coordination.

The system supports cybersecurity observation, threat detection, and resilience planning for institutions and critical infrastructure.

---

## Core Engines

CYBER•32 operates through four analytical engines.

**THREAT•32**  
Cyber threat detection and pattern analysis.

**SURFACE•32**  
Attack surface mapping and vulnerability exposure analysis.

**RESILIENCE•32**  
Infrastructure resilience and recovery capability evaluation.

**INCIDENT•32**  
Incident response monitoring and response coordination analysis.

Together these engines allow institutions to evaluate cyber risk through a structural lens rather than isolated security alerts.
