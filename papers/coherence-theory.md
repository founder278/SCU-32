# Coherence Theory

## Core Principle

Coherence determines whether a system remains inside its validity domain.

When coherence degrades, systems accumulate structural friction, correction energy, and decision-outcome drift.

## Structural Variables

- System strain
- Structural friction
- Correction energy
- Decision-outcome drift
- Intervention latency
- Incentive misalignment

## Implication

Coherence is not aesthetic alignment.

It is a viability constraint that governs whether a system can continue to function without accumulating destructive internal tension.
