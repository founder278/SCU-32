# EDU•32 Framework

Educational Intelligence System  
A structured framework for knowledge transmission, learning systems, and educational architecture.

---

## Overview

EDU•32 focuses on the organization and transmission of knowledge.

Education systems transform structured knowledge into human capability. EDU•32 models learning environments as dynamic systems where information, cognition, and practice interact.

---

## Core Engines

EDU•32 operates through four engines.

**LEARNING•32**  
Learning models and cognitive engagement.

**CURRICULUM•32**  
Knowledge structure and educational design.

**EVALUATION•32**  
Assessment and performance analysis.

**DEVELOPMENT•32**  
Human capability growth and skill formation.
