# NATURA•32 Framework

Natural Medicine Intelligence System  
A structured framework for botanical medicine, traditional healing systems, and natural therapeutic analysis.

---

## Overview

NATURA•32 focuses on natural medicine and botanical therapeutic systems.

Traditional medicine, herbal pharmacology, and plant-based healing practices have supported human health for thousands of years. NATURA•32 organizes these knowledge systems into structured analytical layers that allow natural therapies to be studied, evaluated, and integrated with modern health systems.

The framework assists practitioners in understanding plant-based medicine, natural therapeutic protocols, and ecological health relationships.

---

## Core Engines

NATURA•32 operates through four analytical engines.

**BOTANICAL•32**  
Medicinal plants, phytochemistry, and botanical pharmacology.

**TRADITIONAL•32**  
Traditional healing systems and cultural medical practices.

**THERAPEUTIC•32**  
Natural treatment protocols and therapeutic strategies.

**ECOLOGY•32**  
Relationship between natural environments, plant systems, and human health.

Together these engines allow natural medicine systems to be analyzed as integrated biological and ecological frameworks.
