# PSYCHE•32 Framework

Psychological Insight & Screening Engine  
A structural framework for psychological pattern observation and cognitive state assessment.

---

## Overview

PSYCHE•32 proposes a structured approach to psychological insight through pattern recognition and behavioral signal analysis.

Rather than functioning as a clinical diagnostic system, PSYCHE•32 operates as a non-diagnostic screening and observation framework designed to identify structural indicators of psychological stress, cognitive overload, emotional imbalance, and organizational burnout.

The system supports psychological awareness and research through structured analytical models.

---

## Core Engines

PSYCHE•32 operates through four analytical engines.

**COGNITION•32**  
Cognitive pattern analysis and reasoning structure observation.

**EMOTION•32**  
Emotional signal pattern detection.

**STRESS•32**  
Psychological stress and overload indicators.

**RESILIENCE•32**  
Adaptive stability and recovery pattern observation.

Together these engines allow psychological states to be evaluated through structured signals rather than isolated symptoms.

Computational Psychology.
