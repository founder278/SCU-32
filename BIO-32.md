# BIO•32 Framework

Biological Systems Intelligence  
A structured framework for the analysis of living systems, biological processes, and organism-level dynamics.

---

## Overview

BIO•32 focuses on biological systems as complex adaptive networks.

Living organisms operate through interconnected biological processes including cellular activity, genetic expression, metabolic pathways, and ecological interaction. BIO•32 organizes these biological relationships into structured analytical layers that allow living systems to be studied as coherent dynamic systems.

The framework supports scientific analysis of biological processes across multiple scales, from cellular behavior to organism-level adaptation.

---

## Core Engines

BIO•32 operates through four analytical engines.

**CELL•32**  
Cellular systems, biological structures, and intracellular processes.

**GENOME•32**  
Genetic systems, gene expression, and biological inheritance.

**METABOLISM•32**  
Biochemical pathways, energy systems, and metabolic regulation.

**ADAPTATION•32**  
Biological resilience, evolutionary response, and organism adaptation.

Together these engines allow biological systems to be analyzed as integrated living networks.
