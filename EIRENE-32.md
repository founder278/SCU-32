# EIRENE•32 Framework

Environmental Intelligence System  
A structured framework for ecological monitoring, environmental sustainability, and regenerative system analysis.

---

## Overview

EIRENE•32 focuses on environmental intelligence and ecological system analysis.

Natural systems such as forests, water cycles, soil systems, climate patterns, and biodiversity operate as interconnected living networks. EIRENE•32 organizes these ecological relationships into structured layers to support environmental monitoring, sustainability planning, and regenerative environmental strategies.

The framework enables environmental operators to analyze ecosystem stability, environmental risk, and long-term ecological resilience.

---

## Core Engines

EIRENE•32 operates through four environmental engines.

**ECO•32**  
Ecosystem structure, biodiversity systems, and ecological balance.

**CLIMATE•32**  
Climate dynamics, environmental conditions, and atmospheric interaction.

**WATER•32**  
Hydrological systems, watersheds, and water cycle monitoring.

**REGEN•32**  
Ecological restoration, regenerative systems, and environmental recovery strategies.

Together these engines allow environmental systems to be analyzed as coherent living infrastructures.
