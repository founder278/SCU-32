# DATA•32 Framework

Data Systems Intelligence  
A structured framework for data acquisition, analysis, and knowledge extraction.

---

## Overview

DATA•32 focuses on the organization and interpretation of information across systems.

Scientific, technical, and institutional systems rely on reliable data structures. DATA•32 provides the analytical infrastructure required to transform raw data into structured knowledge.

---

## Core Engines

DATA•32 operates through four engines.

**COLLECT•32**  
Data acquisition and system inputs.

**PROCESS•32**  
Data cleaning, transformation, and structuring.

**ANALYZE•32**  
Pattern detection and analytical modeling.

**KNOWLEDGE•32**  
Conversion of analyzed data into decision support.
