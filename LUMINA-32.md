# LUMINA•32 Framework

Creative Intelligence System  
A structured framework for visual creativity, artistic expression, and narrative communication.

---

## Overview

LUMINA•32 explores the intersection between creativity and intelligent systems.

Creative processes such as visual design, narrative construction, artistic production, and symbolic communication can be understood as structured systems of expression. LUMINA•32 organizes these processes into analyzable layers to support creative development and cultural communication.

The framework assists creators, designers, and communicators in developing coherent visual and narrative systems.

---

## Core Engines

LUMINA•32 operates through four creative engines.

**VISION•32**  
Concept development, creative ideation, and symbolic imagination.

**DESIGN•32**  
Visual composition, aesthetic coherence, and visual structure.

**NARRATIVE•32**  
Story structure, message clarity, and communication flow.

**PRODUCTION•32**  
Media creation, digital content generation, and artistic execution.

Together these engines allow creative work to evolve as structured expressive systems.
