# ARCHITECT•32 Framework

Architectural Intelligence System  
A structural architecture for spatial design analysis, building systems reasoning, and architectural decision support.

---

## Overview

ARCHITECT•32 proposes a structured approach to architectural intelligence.

Rather than treating architecture as isolated drawings or visual design, the framework organizes spatial logic, functional relationships, building systems, environmental conditions, and design constraints into a coherent analytical structure.

The system is designed to support architectural reasoning, design development, and built-environment decision processes.

---

## Core Engines

ARCHITECT•32 operates through four analytical engines.

**SPACE•32**  
Spatial organization, circulation logic, and layout structure.

**FORM•32**  
Architectural form analysis and design coherence.

**SYSTEMS•32**  
Building systems integration including mechanical, structural, and environmental considerations.

**SITE•32**  
Site conditions, orientation, climate influence, and contextual integration.

Together these engines allow architectural projects to be evaluated as coherent spatial systems.
