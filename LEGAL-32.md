# LEGAL•32 Framework

Legal Intelligence & Governance System  
A structural architecture for legal reasoning support, regulatory analysis, and institutional compliance.

---

## Overview

LEGAL•32 proposes a structured approach to legal intelligence.

Rather than replacing legal professionals, the framework supports legal reasoning by organizing statutory structures, regulatory constraints, contractual dynamics, and compliance requirements into a coherent analytical system.

The system is designed to assist legal research, regulatory navigation, and institutional governance analysis.

---

## Core Engines

LEGAL•32 operates through four analytical engines.

**STATUTE•32**  
Statutory and regulatory structure analysis.

**CONTRACT•32**  
Contract structure and obligation mapping.

**COMPLIANCE•32**  
Regulatory compliance monitoring and governance alignment.

**CASELAW•32**  
Legal precedent pattern recognition and comparative reasoning.

Together these engines support structured legal insight across institutional environments.
