# CIVIL•32 Framework

Civil Engineering Intelligence System  
A structured framework for infrastructure reasoning, structural analysis, and large-scale built environment systems.

---

## Overview

CIVIL•32 proposes a systematic approach to civil engineering intelligence.

Infrastructure systems such as transportation networks, structural systems, water systems, and urban infrastructure operate as complex interconnected systems. CIVIL•32 organizes these systems into analyzable layers to support engineering decisions and infrastructure planning.

The framework allows engineers, planners, and infrastructure operators to evaluate the resilience, coherence, and operational integrity of large-scale civil systems.

---

## Core Engines

CIVIL•32 operates through four analytical engines.

**STRUCTURE•32**  
Structural analysis of buildings, bridges, and engineered systems.

**INFRA•32**  
Infrastructure networks including roads, transportation systems, and urban connectivity.

**WATER•32**  
Hydraulic systems, drainage, water management, and environmental water flows.

**RESILIENCE•32**  
Infrastructure durability, system stress evaluation, and long-term structural sustainability.

Together these engines allow civil engineering projects to be analyzed as integrated infrastructure systems.
